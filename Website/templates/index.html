How to Play?

Space Bio-Superhero is an educational video game, which aims to help people understand space hazards that affects astronauts health. Your mission is to create space super hero from different organs of all organsims, so that it has the power to survive against the space hazards





Who are ASME Flares?

We are a group of students from the faculty of biomedical engineering in cairo university, we're trying to make space research more fun and accessible to the general population.
